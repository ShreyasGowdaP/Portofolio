A sand battery system designed for household usage stores renewable electricity in the form of thermal energy by heating sand, which retains the temperature for extended periods. The system captures energy from renewable sources like solar or wind, then releases stored heat for space heating or hot water, providing an efficient and sustainable way to manage household energy needs.
